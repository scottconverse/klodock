2. What KloDock Is

KloDock is a free, open-source desktop application that provides a graphical interface for the OpenClaw AI agent framework. KloDock helps you install, configure, and interact with an AI agent that runs on your own computer. KloDock is not an AI provider — it connects to third-party AI services that you choose and configure.

6. Data and Privacy

6.1 Local Operation

KloDock operates entirely on your local machine. The KloDock application itself does not transmit any data to any server. There is no KloDock account, no analytics, no telemetry, and no crash reporting.

6.2 Third-Party AI Providers

When you configure a cloud AI provider, your messages are sent to that provider's servers for processing. Each provider has its own privacy policy and data handling practices. KloDock does not control, monitor, or have access to your communications with AI providers. Review your chosen provider's privacy policy before sharing sensitive information.

6.3 API Key Storage

Your API keys are stored in your operating system's native credential store (Windows DPAPI, macOS Keychain, or Linux libsecret). KloDock does not transmit your API keys anywhere. However, KloDock cannot guarantee the security of your API keys against local threats such as malware, physical access to your unlocked computer, or vulnerabilities in your operating system's credential store.

6.4 Ollama (Local AI)

When you use Ollama as your AI provider, all processing happens on your machine. No data is sent to any external service. No API key is stored or required.
